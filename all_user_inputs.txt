3000 Kyun Nahi? Kyunki React framework default me 3000 port use karta hai. Agar aap backend ko bhi 3000 de doge, toh dono aapas me takra jayenge (Port Collision) aur error aayega.

Standard Ports: Industry me backend ke liye standard tarike se 5000, 8000, ya 8080 use kiya jata hai. Aap chaho toh PORT=2000, PORT=4000 ya PORT=9000 bhi rakh sakte ho, koi dikkat nahi hai.
